Frist Steps to TrentU Life.

Check out more details on Github

This project successfully developed an intuitive website designed to assist international students transitioning to Trent University. By consolidating essential information and steps into a single platform, the site guided students through various processes they needed to navigate after accepting their offer, including course registration and utilization of the MyTrent portal. The unique design presented these steps in a game-like, level format, enabling users to track their progress and ensuring that no crucial step was overlooked.

Project Client: Trent Univeristy International Department Head

Project Start Date: September 15, 2022

Project End Date: November 23, 2022

Project Methodology: Agile

Project Planning

The project began with an initial sprint that focused on defining the scope, gathering requirements, and planning the project's features. Following this, four subsequent sprints were organized, each lasting two weeks. These sprints allowed for the phased release of key features, ensuring a systematic approach to development. During this process, critical functionalities were implemented, including applications for study permits, email activation, housing applications, and guidance on course registration. Regular feedback sessions with the client, Paul Longhurst, and Trent International facilitated continuous improvement, allowing for the addition and testing of new features with each release.

Bi-weekly feedback sessions with the client ensured that the website met the needs of international students. This ongoing dialogue fostered a collaborative environment, allowing the team to refine features and enhance user experience, ultimately leading to a polished final product.
