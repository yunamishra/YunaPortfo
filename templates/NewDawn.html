New Dawn.

Check out more details on Github

New Dawn emerged from a growing concern regarding the visibility and welfare of the homeless population in cold urban environments. Observations of individuals seeking refuge in parks during harsh winter conditions inspired the creation of this project, which aims to bridge the gap between the community and those in need.

Project Vision

According to the 2021 Canada Census, thousands of individuals were identified as homeless, highlighting a pressing social issue. To facilitate meaningful support from the public, it is essential to provide an accessible way to locate and understand the needs of this population.
New Dawn aims to develop a web application that serves as a comprehensive resource for the homeless community. This platform will enable users to upload information about homeless individuals, including their locations and specific needs. By doing so, it not only gives a voice to this often-overlooked community but also empowers those who wish to assist them.

  • To create an intuitive platform that allows the public to easily upload information about individuals in need.
  • To incorporate geolocation features that facilitate localized assistance.
  • To provide updated information on nearby shelters and resources.
  • To ensure a successful launch and widespread adoption of the web application.

Objectives

The project focuses on developing a user-friendly web application for public contributions regarding the homeless, enabling account creation for personalized engagement, integrating geolocation capabilities, and including feedback features for users who provide assistance. By addressing these objectives, New Dawn aims to contribute to reducing poverty rates, help decrease incidents of theft and related crimes, create a unified platform for discovering and supporting the homeless population, and improve resource efficiency by redirecting items to those in need. New Dawn seeks to illuminate the path toward a more compassionate and informed society, where the homeless community can find the support and recognition they deserve.
